1. Intro

This website's purpose is to help and guide young magician who are just discovering the world on magic. On this website you will learn the difference between some kind of perfomence styles.

2. Stage Magic


Stage Magic, is a kind of magic that is performed only on the stage. The main reason we make this division, because some tricks require quite a few special details. For example most of those tricks need a lot of space, need the spectator remain in front of you and require quite large equipment. Like a large cage or an aquarium.


3. Street Magic

Street Magic is a section between stage and close-up because they are performed for a small group of people but more that three. Moreover, usually it take place outside, but those can be executed at any place like home, bar or a party. A particularity about this category is those tricks are universal, therefore, it’s possible to perform them in almost any situation.

4. Close-Up Magic

Close-Up, is the most common nowadays because for those kind of tricks it’s way easier to find an audience. Another bonus is that you don’t have to carry a lot of gimmicks on you and mostly object that you can borrow.
